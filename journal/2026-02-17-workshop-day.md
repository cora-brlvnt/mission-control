## Lessons from Pre-Workshop Preparation

### Lesson 1: Practical Materials Win Over Polish
- 14.5 KB tutorial guide (copy-paste ready) > fancy UI
- Reason: Attendees want immediate usability, not aesthetics
- Application: For FastTrack Hub, prioritize "what can I do right now" over "beautiful dashboard"

### Lesson 2: Demonstrate, Don't Explain
- "Show 2 workflows live" > "explain 5 workflows in slides"
- Reason: Humans trust what they see; theory is forgettable
- Application: Every follow-up should include a small, real action (code snippet, workflow template, prompt)

### Lesson 3: Validate Before Spending
- Run GHL on $0 Starter plan first > upgrade to $497/mo Agency Pro immediately
- Reason: Market validation proves ROI before commitment
- Application: All major tools/spends: demand customer proof before spending (workshops generate leads that justify tech spend)

### Lesson 4: Let Systems Do the Work
- Automated cron jobs run during travel > manual updates after return
- Reason: Reduces decision fatigue; keeps momentum alive
- Application: Every successful cron job = one less bottleneck when Renzo returns
