### 3. Window-Based Planning (Mechanical Constraints)
- **Core principle:** Sprint boundaries create non-negotiable hard deadlines
- **Cascade rule:** Missing a gate doesn't compress into current window; it cascades into next planning cycle
- **March 27 case study:** Soft March 17 deadline → cascades to April 1 re-plan if missed
- **Solution:** Enforce gates early with escalation points (Day 3 of slip → escalate)

- **Effectiveness:** Compression of decision time by 5–7x vs. open-ended format
