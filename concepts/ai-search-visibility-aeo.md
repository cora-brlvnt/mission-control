# AI Search Visibility (AEO — Answer Engine Optimization)

**Source:** Daily AI Marketing Brief, April 9, 2026
**Status:** Research finding / Service opportunity

---

## Core Insight
The shift from traditional SEO to AI search visibility (AEO) is the biggest underserved gap in B2B marketing right now. Average B2B maturity is only 3.2/5.

## What It Is
- **AEO** = optimizing content to appear in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- Different from SEO: AI engines synthesize answers from multiple sources rather than ranking pages
- Brands need to be the *source* AI pulls from, not just rank on page 1

## Why It Matters for Berelvant
1. **Zero cost to execute** — 3-4 hours to build an AI Visibility Audit service
2. **Dual output** — produces both a new service line AND LinkedIn content
3. **Positioning fit** — aligns with "growth architect" brand (infrastructure, not hype)
4. **Differentiation** — most agencies still selling traditional SEO; AEO is white space
5. **Client relevance** — Forex.com and fintech clients need discoverability in AI search

## Potential Service: AI Visibility Audit
- Audit how a brand appears in AI-generated answers
- Benchmark against competitors in AI search results
- Identify content gaps that AI engines can't find/cite
- Deliver actionable roadmap for AEO optimization
