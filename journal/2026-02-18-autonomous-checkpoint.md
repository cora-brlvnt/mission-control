**Impact:** Eliminates 90% of clarifying questions.

- Think 10 steps ahead: what could break?
- Never skip full pass review

**Impact:** Catch errors before they reach clients. Trust > Speed.

### Lesson 3: Pragmatism Over Bureaucracy
Build as you go. Choose what works now + costs nothing.

**Impact:** Removes decision bottleneck. Renzo's time freed for strategy.
